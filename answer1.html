Identify Cognitive Distortions

Jure has a good relationship with his undergraduate student. The student always comes in time, listens carefully to the instructions, takes notes and asks questions when unclear. Jure created a timeline to help his undergraduate student to prepare the research paper stress-free and ahead of time. The student missed all the deadlines Jure suggested and turned in the paper a week before the official submission deadline. Jure thought:

This is the part of human development. What was I doing as an undergraduate student? Pretty much the same thing! A lot of coursework, a lot of research, a lot of assignment, very little experience in managing time on your own.

No distortion

Jury could have suspected the student of being disrespectful, blame himself of being a bad advisor, but would it help get the research paper faster? I doubt it.
