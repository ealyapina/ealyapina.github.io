Identify Cognitive Distortions

Elvia had thawed her sample and started processing it. The protocol requires to use an ultracentrifuge, when it was time to use it, Elvia discovered that three previous users encountered an error which prevented the centrifuge from running, logged the error into the log book and told no one about the problem. She never encountered that situation before. Elvia had to discard her sample. She thought:

I should have checked every resource for my experiment the day before

"Should" statement

It would be nice if Elvia checked everything before, but she had not experienced that situation before and had no knowledge if it was necessary. Moreover, guilt does not help the experiment. Let's help her rephrase her unhelpful thought: "Next time I will check resources for my experiment the day before and adjust my plans correspondingly."
